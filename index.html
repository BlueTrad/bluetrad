Oil Products

Direct refinery access to oil products at locations in Europe, the Black Sea, and the Mideast Gulf.

Customers active in Europe, north and west Africa, and the Black Sea - with a focus on heavy-end products.

Operating specialised tankers of 4,000 deadweight tonnes and 15,000 deadweight tonnes for cargoes into the Black Sea and African ports.

Land logistics including rail and heated or flatbed trucks to customers across Europe, as well as a small number of bitutainers that we send to Africa and South America.

Construction Materials

Network of steel, cement, and aggregates suppliers in the Baltics and the Mideast Gulf.

Supplying constructors and local dealers the materials for large commercial projects including highways, stadiums, and shopping malls.

Working alongside our customers to ensure timely deliveries that best fit their programmes.

We have in 2024 begun the supply of Fly Ash to customers in Europe from Asia in vessels up to Handymax.

Agriculture

Wide network of experienced brokers and originators at the major European and Black Sea hubs for grains and meals.

Primarily focused on feed grains and meals, working at origin in Brazil, CVB, Turkey, and the Baltics. Loading of coasters up to 5,000 deadweight, as well as rail and truck deliveries.

Customers include state purchasing agencies, and feed mills in the Mideast Gulf, and Africa.

Softs

Farm to roaster supply of Vietnamese green Arabica and Robusta beans from single containers.

Source various grades to match our customers requirements. Network of farm traders in Kenya, Tanzania, Malawi, and Rwanda.

Shipments of both containerised white sugar, and the brokerage of larger cargoes up to Panamax for customers in the Mideast Gulf.

Logistics management that enables us to deliver efficiently even amid volatile container, sugar, and coffee global markets.
